---
name: fix-rebase
description: Navigate and resolve active git rebase conflicts with a focus on logical intent over simple text merging.
---

I am currently in a rebase and have hit conflicts. Your goal is to act as a partner in resolving these by analyzing the conflicting blocks. Don't just pick "ours" or "theirs"—look at the logic of both sides to understand what each was trying to achieve. Find all files in a conflicted state, explain the nature of the collision to me, and propose resolutions that maintain the integrity of both my new changes and the upstream updates.
